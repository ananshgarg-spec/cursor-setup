## Post 2

**Date:** December 2025 *(based on the LinkedIn activity timestamp)*

**Original Link:**  
[https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aleyda_fan-out-framework-5-steps-to-improve-seo-activity-7477376945052262400-1b5W](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aleyda_fan-out-framework-5-steps-to-improve-seo-activity-7477376945052262400-1b5W)

### Summary

Aleyda introduces the **Fan-Out Framework**, a structured methodology for improving SEO content performance by expanding a core topic into multiple supporting content pieces. The framework focuses on identifying search intent, creating comprehensive topic coverage, strengthening internal linking, and improving content organization. She explains that this approach helps build topical authority while making content easier for both users and search engines to understand.

### Main SEO Takeaway

- Expand one primary topic into multiple supporting pages to build topical authority.
- Organize content around user intent instead of isolated keywords.
- Strengthen internal linking between related content.
- Comprehensive topic coverage improves crawlability, relevance, and long-term organic visibility.
